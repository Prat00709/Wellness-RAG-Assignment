Title: How to Choose the Right Yoga Practice
Category: Guidance
Level: All Levels

Overview:
Choosing the right yoga practice depends on individual goals, experience, and
physical condition.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Starting yoga
- Returning after a break
- Managing specific needs

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Choosing advanced practices prematurely

Key Benefits:
- Improves practice suitability
- Reduces injury risk

Practice Notes:
Beginners should prioritize foundational and gentle practices.

Common Questions Answered:
- How do I know which practice is right? Start simple.
- Can practices change over time? Yes.

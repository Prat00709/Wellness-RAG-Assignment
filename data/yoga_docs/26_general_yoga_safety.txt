Title: General Yoga Safety Guidelines
Category: Safety & Wellness
Level: All Levels

Overview:
General safety guidelines help ensure yoga is practiced in a safe, mindful, and
sustainable manner.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- For all practitioners
- Before starting new routines
- During self-guided practice

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Ignoring pain signals
- Practicing without rest

Key Benefits:
- Reduces injury risk
- Encourages mindful practice

Practice Notes:
Pain, dizziness, or breath discomfort are signals to stop immediately.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is yoga always safe? No, awareness is required.
- Should pain be ignored? No.

- Contraindications in Yoga
- Yoga After Injury

Title: Trikonasana (Triangle Pose)
Category: Asana
Level: Beginner

Overview:
A standing pose emphasizing lateral stretch and alignment.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Improving hip flexibility
- Balance training

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Lower back pain

Key Benefits:
- Stretches legs and spine

Practice Notes:
Keep chest open, spine long.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is it beginner-friendly? Yes, with support.

Related Practices:
- Tadasana
- Virabhadrasana II

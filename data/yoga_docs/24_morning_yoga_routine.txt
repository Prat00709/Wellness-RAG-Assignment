Title: Morning Yoga Routine
Category: Wellness & Lifestyle
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Morning yoga routines are designed to gently awaken the body and prepare it for
daily activities.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- To increase morning energy
- To reduce stiffness after sleep
- To establish a daily routine

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Rushing through movements
- Practicing intensely without warm-up

Key Benefits:
- Improves circulation
- Enhances alertness
- Builds consistency

Practice Notes:
Movements should be slow and mindful. Breathing should remain comfortable.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is morning yoga better than evening? It depends on preference.
- Should it be intense? No.

Related Practices:
- Surya Namaskar
- Tadasana

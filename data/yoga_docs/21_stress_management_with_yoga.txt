Title: Stress Management with Yoga
Category: Wellness & Lifestyle
Level: All Levels

Overview:
Yoga is commonly used as a supportive practice for managing stress through
movement, breathing, and relaxation techniques.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- During periods of mental or emotional stress
- To promote relaxation and calmness
- As part of a balanced wellness routine

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Expecting yoga to replace professional mental health care
- Practicing forcefully during emotional distress

Key Benefits:
- Reduces stress response
- Encourages relaxation
- Improves emotional awareness

Practice Notes:
Gentle practices, breathing awareness, and relaxation poses are most effective
for stress management.

Common Questions Answered:
- Can yoga cure stress? No, but it can help manage it.
- Is daily practice beneficial? Yes, gently.

Related Practices:
- Shavasana
- Bhramari Pranayama

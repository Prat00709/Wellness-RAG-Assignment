Title: Yoga for Better Sleep
Category: Wellness & Lifestyle
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Yoga can support better sleep by promoting relaxation and reducing physical and
mental tension before bedtime.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- For difficulty falling asleep
- To create a calming bedtime routine
- To reduce nighttime restlessness

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Vigorous practices close to bedtime
- Forcing stretches when fatigued

Key Benefits:
- Improves sleep quality
- Encourages relaxation
- Reduces restlessness

Practice Notes:
Slow movements, gentle stretches, and breathing practices are recommended in
the evening.

Common Questions Answered:
- Can yoga replace sleep medication? No.
- When should it be practiced? In the evening, gently.

Related Practices:
- Balasana
- Shavasana
- Anulom Vilom

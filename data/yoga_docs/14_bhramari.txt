Title: Bhramari Pranayama (Humming Bee Breath)
Category: Pranayama
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Bhramari is a calming breathing practice that involves producing a gentle
humming sound during exhalation. It is commonly used to reduce mental agitation.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- During stress or anxiety
- Before sleep
- To calm the mind

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Excessive pressure on ears
- Severe ear infections

Key Benefits:
- Calms the nervous system
- Reduces stress
- Improves focus

Practice Notes:
The humming sound should remain soft and comfortable. No strain should be felt
in the throat or ears.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is it safe for daily practice? Yes.
- Does it require breath holding? No.

Related Practices:
- Anulom Vilom
- Shavasana

Title: Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose)
Category: Asana
Level: Beginner

Overview:
A gentle backbend performed lying on the abdomen.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Improving spinal mobility
- Countering prolonged sitting

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Pregnancy
- Recent spinal injury

Key Benefits:
- Strengthens spine
- Opens chest

Practice Notes:
Lift using back muscles, not arms.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is it safe daily? Yes, pain-free.

Related Practices:
- Setu Bandhasana

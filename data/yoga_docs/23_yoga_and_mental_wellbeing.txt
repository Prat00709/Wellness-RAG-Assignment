Title: Yoga and Mental Well-being
Category: Wellness & Lifestyle
Level: All Levels

Overview:
Yoga supports mental well-being by encouraging mindfulness, breathing awareness,
and gentle physical activity.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- During periods of mental fatigue
- To support emotional regulation
- As a complementary wellness practice

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Using yoga as a substitute for mental health treatment
- Ignoring persistent mental health concerns

Key Benefits:
- Encourages mindfulness
- Reduces mental tension
- Improves emotional awareness

Practice Notes:
Consistency and gentleness are key. Professional support should be sought when
needed.

Common Questions Answered:
- Can yoga treat mental illness? No.
- Can it support well-being? Yes.

Related Practices:
- Meditation
- Pranayama Basics

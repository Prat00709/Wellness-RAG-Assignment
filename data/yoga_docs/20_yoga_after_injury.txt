Title: Yoga After Injury
Category: Safety & Wellness
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Yoga after injury can support recovery by encouraging gentle movement and body
awareness. However, it must be approached carefully to avoid re-injury.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- During rehabilitation with approval
- For regaining mobility gradually
- Under guided supervision

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Acute pain or inflammation
- Ignoring medical advice
- Returning to advanced poses too soon

Key Benefits:
- Supports gentle mobility
- Encourages mindful movement
- Aids recovery awareness

Practice Notes:
Pain is a signal to stop. Movements should be slow, controlled, and adapted to
individual recovery stages.

Common Questions Answered:
- Can yoga speed up recovery? It may support it gently.
- Should pain be pushed through? No.

Related Practices:
- Balasana
- Gentle Stretching

Title: Common Beginner Mistakes in Yoga
Category: Guidance
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Beginners often make mistakes related to effort, comparison, and expectations.
Recognizing these helps build a safer and more enjoyable practice.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- When starting yoga
- To build sustainable habits

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Comparing progress with others
- Pushing for perfection

Key Benefits:
- Reduces frustration
- Prevents injury

Practice Notes:
Yoga progress is individual and non-competitive.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is flexibility required? No.
- Should poses look perfect? No.

- Yoga for Beginners
- General Yoga Safety

Title: Yoga for Beginners
Category: Practice Guidance
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Yoga for beginners focuses on building awareness, comfort, and consistency
rather than advanced poses. The goal is to establish a safe and sustainable
practice.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Starting yoga for the first time
- Returning after a long break
- Building confidence in basic poses

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Comparing progress with others
- Pushing into discomfort or pain

Key Benefits:
- Improves flexibility and balance
- Builds foundational strength
- Encourages mindful movement

Practice Notes:
Beginners should prioritize learning alignment and breathing over complexity.
Guidance is recommended when possible.

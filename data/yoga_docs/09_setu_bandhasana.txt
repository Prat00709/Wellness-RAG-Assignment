Title: Setu Bandhasana (Bridge Pose)
Category: Asana
Level: Beginner

Overview:
A gentle backbend strengthening the back and legs.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Improving spinal flexibility

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Neck injuries

Key Benefits:
- Strengthens back
- Opens chest

Practice Notes:
Avoid over-arching.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is it safe for beginners? Yes.

Related Practices:
- Bhujangasana

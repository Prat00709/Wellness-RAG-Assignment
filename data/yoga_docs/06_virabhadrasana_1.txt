Title: Virabhadrasana I (Warrior I)
Category: Asana
Level: Intermediate

Overview:
A strong standing pose building stability and focus.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Strength training
- Building endurance

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Knee or lower back pain

Key Benefits:
- Strengthens legs and core

Practice Notes:

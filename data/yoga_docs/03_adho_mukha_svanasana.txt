Title: Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward Facing Dog)
Category: Asana
Level: Beginner

Overview:
A commonly used yoga posture combining strength and stretching.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Stretching the back body
- Flow sequences

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Wrist or shoulder pain
- Uncontrolled high blood pressure

Key Benefits:
- Stretches legs and shoulders
- Strengthens arms

Practice Notes:
Lengthen spine, relax neck.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is it safe for beginners? Yes, with modifications.

Related Practices:
- Balasana
- Surya Namaskar

Title: Yoga and Medical Conditions
Category: Safety & Wellness
Level: All Levels

Overview:
Yoga may support general well-being but should be practiced cautiously in the
presence of medical conditions.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- As a complementary wellness practice
- Under medical or professional guidance

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Using yoga as a replacement for treatment
- Practicing without disclosure of conditions

Key Benefits:
- Encourages mindful movement
- Supports relaxation

Practice Notes:
Medical advice should always take priority over yoga guidance.

Common Questions Answered:
- Can yoga cure diseases? No.
- Should doctors be consulted? Yes.

Related Practices:
- Yoga for High Blood Pressure
- Yoga After Injury

Title: Kapalbhati Pranayama
Category: Pranayama
Level: Intermediate

Overview:
Kapalbhati is a breathing practice involving active exhalations and passive
inhalations. It is traditionally used to promote alertness and abdominal
engagement.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- For experienced practitioners
- As part of energizing routines
- Under guided instruction

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Pregnancy
- Hernia or abdominal surgery
- High blood pressure or heart conditions

Key Benefits:
- Improves abdominal awareness
- Increases alertness
- Supports breathing efficiency

Practice Notes:
The practice should never be forceful. Guidance is strongly recommended,
especially for beginners.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is Kapalbhati safe during pregnancy? No.
- Can beginners practice it? Only under guidance.

Title: Advanced Yoga Practices
Category: Practice Guidance
Level: Advanced

Overview:
Advanced yoga practices involve complex postures, increased strength, and
greater body awareness. These practices are suitable only after building a
strong foundation.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- After consistent foundational practice
- Under professional supervision
- For experienced practitioners

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Beginners without guidance
- During injury or illness

Key Benefits:
- Enhances strength and flexibility
- Improves control and endurance
- Deepens focus and discipline

Practice Notes:
Proper warm-up and recovery are essential. Advanced practices should never be
forced.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is advanced yoga necessary? No.
- Can it increase injury risk? Yes, without guidance.

Related Practices:
- Virabhadrasana I
- Surya Namaskar

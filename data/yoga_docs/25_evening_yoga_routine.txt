Title: Evening Yoga Routine
Category: Wellness & Lifestyle
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Evening yoga routines focus on relaxation and releasing tension accumulated
during the day.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- After a long day
- Before sleep
- To reduce muscle tension

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Vigorous or stimulating poses
- Overstretching tired muscles

Key Benefits:
- Promotes relaxation
- Improves sleep readiness
- Reduces physical tension

Practice Notes:
Slow pacing and calming practices are preferred in the evening.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is evening yoga safe daily? Yes, gently.
- Should breathing be slow? Yes.

Related Practices:
- Balasana
- Shavasana

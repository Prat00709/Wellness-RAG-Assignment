Title: Pranayama Basics
Category: Breathing Practice
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Pranayama refers to yogic breathing practices designed to cultivate awareness
and regulation of the breath. These practices are foundational in yoga and are
often used to support relaxation and focus.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- For beginners learning breath awareness
- During stress or mental fatigue
- As preparation for meditation

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Breath retention without guidance
- Practicing when dizzy or unwell

Key Benefits:
- Improves breathing awareness
- Promotes calmness
- Supports mental clarity

Practice Notes:
Breathing should always remain comfortable and unforced. Smooth inhalation and
exhalation are more important than duration.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is pranayama safe for beginners? Yes, when practiced gently.
- Should breath be held? Not without guidance.

Related Practices:
- Anulom Vilom
- Bhramari Pranayama

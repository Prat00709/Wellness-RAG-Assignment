Title: Yoga During Pregnancy
Category: Safety & Wellness
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Yoga during pregnancy can support gentle movement, relaxation, and breathing
awareness. However, pregnancy involves significant physical changes that require
special care.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- For gentle mobility during pregnancy
- For relaxation and stress management
- Under professional supervision

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Deep twists, strong backbends, and inversions
- Balance-intensive poses without support
- Practicing without guidance

Key Benefits:
- Encourages gentle movement
- Supports breathing awareness
- Promotes relaxation

Practice Notes:
Only pregnancy-safe practices should be used. Any discomfort is a signal to
stop immediately.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is yoga safe during pregnancy? Only with modifications.
- Can all poses be practiced? No.

Related Practices:
- Prenatal Yoga
- Gentle Pranayama

Summarized from public prenatal yoga and wellness education sources.

Title: Tadasana (Mountain Pose)
Category: Asana
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Tadasana is a foundational standing posture used to develop proper posture,
balance, and body awareness.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Learning correct posture
- Starting standing sequences

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Balance difficulties without support

Key Benefits:
- Improves posture
- Enhances balance

Practice Notes:
Weight evenly distributed, calm breathing.

Common Questions Answered:
- Is it suitable for beginners? Yes.

Related Practices:
- Trikonasana
- Virabhadrasana I

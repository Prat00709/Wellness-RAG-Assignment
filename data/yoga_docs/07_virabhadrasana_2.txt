Title: Virabhadrasana II (Warrior II)
Category: Asana
Level: Intermediate

Overview:
A standing posture focusing on stamina and alignment.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- Improving leg strength
- Mental focus

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Knee discomfort

Key Benefits:
- Builds stamina
- Improves posture

Practice Notes:
Gaze steady, shoulders relaxed.

Common Questions Answered:
- Can it improve balance? Yes.

Related Practices:
- Trikonasana

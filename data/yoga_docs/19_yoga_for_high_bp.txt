Title: Yoga for High Blood Pressure
Category: Safety & Wellness
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Yoga may support relaxation and stress management, which can be helpful for
overall well-being. Individuals with high blood pressure should practice with
caution.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- For relaxation-focused routines
- Under professional guidance
- As a complementary wellness practice

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Breath retention practices
- Inversions or forceful techniques
- Practicing without medical advice

Key Benefits:
- Encourages calm breathing
- Supports relaxation
- Reduces stress response

Practice Notes:
Slow, gentle movements and breathing are preferred. Monitoring comfort is
essential.

Common Questions Answered:
- Can yoga replace medication? No.
- Is all yoga safe for high BP? No.

Related Practices:
- Gentle Pranayama
- Shavasana

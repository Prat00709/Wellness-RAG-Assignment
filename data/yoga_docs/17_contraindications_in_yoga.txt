Title: Contraindications in Yoga
Category: Safety & Wellness
Level: All Levels

Overview:
Contraindications in yoga refer to situations where certain poses or practices
should be avoided or modified due to health conditions or physical limitations.
Understanding contraindications is essential for safe practice.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- When choosing appropriate yoga practices
- Before starting a new pose or routine
- For preventing injury

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- During illness, injury, or recovery
- With conditions such as hernia, glaucoma, or joint instability
- Without professional guidance in medical cases

Key Benefits:
- Reduces risk of injury
- Encourages informed practice
- Promotes long-term safety

Practice Notes:
Yoga should never cause pain. Modifications and alternatives should always be
used when necessary.

Common Questions Answered:
- Are all yoga poses safe for everyone? No.
- Should medical conditions be disclosed to instructors? Yes.

Title: Anulom Vilom (Alternate Nostril Breathing)
Category: Pranayama
Level: Beginner

Overview:
Anulom Vilom is a breathing technique involving alternate nostrils. It is widely
used to promote calmness and balance within the nervous system.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- During stress or anxiety
- Before sleep or meditation
- To encourage rhythmic breathing

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Nasal congestion
- Forcing the breath
- Breath retention without instruction

Key Benefits:
- Reduces stress
- Improves breathing rhythm
- Enhances focus

Practice Notes:
Breathing should remain slow and gentle. Comfort is prioritized over duration.

Common Questions Answered:
- Can it be practiced daily? Yes.
- Is it suitable for all ages? Generally yes.

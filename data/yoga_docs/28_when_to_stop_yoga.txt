Title: When to Stop or Modify Yoga Practice
Category: Safety & Wellness
Level: All Levels

Overview:
Knowing when to stop or modify yoga practice is essential for preventing injury
and maintaining long-term well-being.

When This Practice Is Useful:
- During discomfort or pain
- When experiencing illness or fatigue

When To Avoid / Use Caution:
- Pushing through pain
- Ignoring warning signs

Key Benefits:
- Prevents injury
- Encourages body awareness

Practice Notes:
Rest is a valid and important part of yoga practice.

Common Questions Answered:
- Should pain be pushed through? No.
- Is rest part of yoga? Yes.

Related Practices:
- Shavasana
- Balasana
